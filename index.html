  • ABOUT ME

     

    This portfolio has been produced to illustrate my skill as a digital designer. I hope you find it to be a straight forward demonstration of my ability.

     

    I am a positive, accomplished creative who has recently designed beautiful interfaces for local government departments in Wellington, and challenged my ability to learn and problem solve at Enspiral Dev Academy, whilst also nurturing a passion for social enterprise.

     

    Born in Malawi, I have lived in Northern Ireland, and England. I’ve been lucky enough to travel within Europe, Asia, and Australia, a desire to be closer to family brought me to New Zealand. Starting a new life in Wellington has been the most exciting adventure. I have lived in the ‘Coolest Little Capital’ for 10 years.

     

    Anchored here by my fiancé, four chooks, and new puppy Cleo Noodle-Butt, I am a complete convert to ‘brunch’ as the most important meal of the week and can discuss the finer points of hipster coffee and salt-pod float tanks. I put it down to escaping London for wonderful Wellington. I have a new lease of life and find myself totally inspired by the amazing people I have met here.

     

    I am most proud to be an adoptive Kiwi.
